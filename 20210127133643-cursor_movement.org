#+filetags: :Emacs:

* Go To a Specific Line in Emacs
  M-g M-g (number of line)


* Move to beginning of the current or next function

  C-M-a

  C-M-e

* Move between statement

  M-a

  M-e

* Select and move up / down one line

  M-shift-a
  
  M-shift-e
