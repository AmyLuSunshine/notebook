#+title: Both Both of
#+filetags: :Grammar:

- tags :: [[id:7028b1b4-2ecd-4363-a5c0-7be30e2a385a][Grammar]]

Use *both of* for personal pronouns:

Both of us speak English.

Use *both* for other nouns:

Both my brothers speak English.
