#+title: Game Analytics

* Terminology

** Telemetry

   data obtained remotely
 
** Game Metrics

   interpretable measures of something related to games 

e.g: user behaviors

- total playtime
- daily active users


* Objectives

- Gain better knowledge of the users -- players. 

- Support decision making
